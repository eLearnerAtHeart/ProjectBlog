Cooking

There is something special about making home-made meals and the work it takes to follow the recipe step by step. There is beauty in the making process and the amount of ingredients. Besides the health benefits, the waiting time can give you an adrenalin boost. I enjoy home made dish like this Pastelo de Platano Maduro, translated to Sweet Plantain Casserole. The casserole usually includes ground meat, some veggies and cheese of your choices. The making process is usually quick and consist of boiling the sweet plantains. Then, mashing them like mash potatoes with a little salt and butter. You want to make sure to cook separately and season the ground meet of your preference such as chicken, beef, or pork. Similar to a lasagna dish you want to layer it in an oven-safe container or casserole dish. The layering order I normally like to follow is sweet plantain, meat with mixed veggies and third mozzarella cheese. Repeat layering to your preference or container capacity. Ensure everything its cooked prior to layering and top it with cheese for 15-25 mins. All set and ready to enjoy.

Dancing

Dancing for me its a form of relaxation and stress release. My every being moves to the sound of the music. Coming from a Caribbean-Dominican Republic background where we are known traditionally for bachata, merengue and salsa, my body moves naturally. My body and soul move to the rhythm of the beat as a form of expressing authenticity. I grew up in a culture where dancing was a norm for social gathering and socializing. Its something people picks up as you grow-up in the “barrios” (neighborhood) of the city or country-side.There was no teacher nor personal trainers. Your body learned with constant practice and observation. My experience with Jazz was something completely different. It was like learning a new language where you have to keep track of every move from leg rises, turns and spin. It requires more work beyond dance count and a thorough understanding of each movement. The final product felt like a theatrical scene.

Biking

Biking is a form of workout and it turns out to be a little more. My mind travels at a 100 mile at the simple act of moving the pedals and directing my gear. The history with this bike is quiet especial since it was gifted. I called it at the right time and place. There is beauty in second hand items. It makes you question did this bike have a positive life span with the previous owner? How much sweat did they put into their workout and did they give the bike the proper care? Like everything in life a well maintained bike can safe you money and expand its life expectancy. Every ride its a new experience.

H2O

Water is life and there is nothing more I enjoy than to sit by the beach. I like to hear the sound of the waves while watching the sunrise or sunset. I tend to release my stress and all the bagged out to the sea. Reflecting on my day and evaluating how I can always be a better me. The ocean has its way of healing any hardship and somehow tends to do deep cleaning on my mind and soul. I also enjoy swimming or just seating by the edges of the beach as the wave pull and push. I have learned that the ocean has a life of its own since all different kind of creatures live under. At the beach its more common to find jellyfish, crabs and some fishing depending on the area. I once stepped in one of those mysteries whole and panic. This taught me that the ocean can only be trusted to a certain extend.
